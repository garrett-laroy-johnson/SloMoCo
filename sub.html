Submissions

SloMoCo is currently accepting submissions for Spring 2021 phase.

Please see the 'CFP' and 'about' pages to help shape your proposal.

You may use the embedded form below to submit, or open it in another window here.

SloMoCo submissions will be reviewed internally by the SloMoCommittee (with aid from members of the MoCo Steering Committee and broader community). Authors do not need to anonymize their submissions. Submissions will be evaluated on the following criteria: 1) the submission meaningfully engages with MoCo and SloMoCo and 2) we can support the actualization of your proposal. If we feel the proposal needs clarification or re-directing regarding either of these criteria, we will email with questions and/or suggestions.
